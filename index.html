Game Instructions
  • Wait for the computer to play a sequence of blinking photos and whale sounds.
  • Mimic the game sequence order by pressing on corresponding photos.
  • After each round, the game will generate the previous sequence plus one additional photo/sound.
  • YOU WIN after 20 rounds!
  • GAME OVER if you press a photo out of game sequence.
